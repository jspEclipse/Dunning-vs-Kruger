Thesis: Because people are bad at approximating their own ability or knowledge at any particular field, much like the chained in the cave, they tend to overestimate or underestimate their own ability by a significant margin. The Dunning-Kruger effect is an explanation to this phenomenon and The Allegory of The Cave is a story that explains the Dunning Kruger Effect using incredible metaphors and a story.


Dunning Kruger Effect Definition: The Dunning-Kruger effect is a cognitive bias where people with low ability, expertise, or experience regarding a certain type of task or area of knowledge tend to overestimate their ability or knowledge and the opposite also being true.
